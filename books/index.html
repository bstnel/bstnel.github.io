The best of the recent wave of books describing the implications of accelerating technology. Sobering narrative of why the AI revolution could result in mass unemployment and inequality, and a call to face the implications proactively.

The most important book on financial independence. Written by founders of the financial independence movement. The book shines light on the often unexamined relationship between people and money.
